Education

2021 – OOUX Academy

I was the first Brazilian to obtain this certificate and become the first Object Oriented UX strategist in Brazil. OOUX is a design philosophy that respects that human mental models are noun-first rather than verb-first. OOUX drastically changed my approach to design problems and helped me uncover and navigate complexity in any project.
